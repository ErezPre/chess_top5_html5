Chess Games Categories

The International Chess Federation (FIDE) governs international chess competition. Each month, FIDE publishes the lists "Top 100 Players", "Top 100 Women", "Top 100 Juniors" and "Top 100 Girls" rankings of countries according to the average rating of their top 10 players and top 10 female players. The Elo rating system is used.

Chess games are divided by FIDE into categories based on the time controls and/or number of moves per player.
Classical games' time control is usually more than an hour per player, rapid - 10-60 min per player, blitz - 1-10 min per player and bullet - less than 1 min per player (there are more categories out there but these are the most popular).
